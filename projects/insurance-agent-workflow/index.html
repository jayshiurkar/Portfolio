P&C Insurance Agent Workflow

AI-enabled workflow design for first notice of loss, claims triage, policy servicing, payment status, and broker-carrier handoffs with human oversight, audit logging, and adoption metrics.

Project Summary

This project converts property and casualty insurance servicing pain points into structured agent requirements, escalation logic, compliance-safe response rules, and operational metrics. The workflow is designed for customer service teams, claims intake teams, brokers, supervisors, and compliance reviewers.

FNOL Claims Triage Policy Servicing Payment Status Broker Handoff Human-in-the-Loop

Core Use Cases

FNOL Intake
Capture incident details, classify loss type, validate required fields, and identify escalation triggers.
Claims Triage
Route claims by severity, urgency, missing documents, coverage uncertainty, and legal involvement.
Policy Servicing
Support policy questions and service tasks while escalating coverage interpretation and regulated actions.
Payment Status
Retrieve billing context, explain payment status, and flag refund disputes or overdue policy risk.
Broker-Carrier Handoff
Summarize request context, package missing documents, and route to the correct carrier team.

Workflow Logic

User Intent → Authentication → Intent Classification → Context Retrieval → Risk and Completeness Checks → Escalation Decision → Agent Response or Human Queue → Audit Log and Metrics

Human-in-the-Loop Controls

  • Escalate coverage uncertainty, injury claims, legal involvement, fraud indicators, and low-confidence outputs.
  • Allow the agent to summarize context, collect missing information, and prepare service tasks without making unauthorized coverage decisions.
  • Capture escalation reasons, human overrides, final resolution status, and SLA timestamps for QA and compliance review.

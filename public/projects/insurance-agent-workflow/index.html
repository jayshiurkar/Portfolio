Project Summary

This project translates insurance servicing pain points into structured agent requirements and workflow logic. The design focuses on safe automation across high-volume customer and broker service interactions while preserving human review for regulated, complex, or high-risk decisions.

FNOL Claims Triage Policy Servicing Payment Status Broker-Carrier Handoff Human-in-the-Loop

Core Use Cases

FNOL Intake

Capture loss details, classify incident type, validate required fields, and trigger escalation for injury, fraud indicators, or coverage uncertainty.

Claims Triage

Route claims by severity, urgency, policy context, document completeness, liability signals, and required handling queue.

Policy Servicing

Support service requests while escalating coverage interpretation, endorsements, cancellations, and regulated disclosures.

Payment Status

Retrieve billing context, explain payment timelines, and flag refund disputes, chargebacks, or overdue policy risk.

Broker-Carrier Handoff

Package request context, summarize next steps, attach missing-field logic, and route to the correct carrier or operations queue.

Workflow Logic

The agent is structured to classify intent, retrieve context, check risk and completeness, decide whether escalation is required, and log the full decision path.

1. User intent received
2. Authenticate user or broker
3. Classify insurance intent
4. Retrieve policy, claim, or billing context
5. Run completeness and risk checks
6. Escalate or respond
7. Log audit trail and metrics

Human-in-the-Loop Controls

Control AreaDesign Decision
Coverage uncertaintyAgent summarizes context but routes final determination to authorized staff.
Fraud or SIU indicatorsAgent flags suspicious patterns and escalates without accusing the customer.
Legal or injury claimsMandatory human review before claim guidance or next-step recommendation.
Low-confidence outputAgent asks clarification questions or escalates with confidence score and reason code.
Complaint riskSensitive interactions route to supervisor queue with full context summary.

Audit Logging and Evaluation

Audit Data

User intent, extracted entities, source context, prompt version, escalation trigger, queue owner, SLA timestamps, human override, and final resolution.

Prompt Evaluation

Intent accuracy, completeness, compliance safety, escalation quality, response clarity, and auditability.

Adoption Metrics

FNOL containment rate, average handling time reduction, first-contact resolution, QA pass rate, human override rate, and broker handoff turnaround.
